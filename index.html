About

I'm a 3D gameplay animator who likes being involved in the full process, from figuring out how a character should move and fight to testing the animation in-game.

I've worked on player characters, enemies, and bosses across a range of projects, and I also rig when a project needs it. Having experience with both helps me understand not only how an animation should look, but how the character needs to function.

What I enjoy most is working closely with other developers, solving problems together, and seeing the animation become part of something that feels good to play.
