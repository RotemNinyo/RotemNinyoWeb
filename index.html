About

I'm Rotem Ninyo, a 3D gameplay animator focused on making characters feel responsive, weighted, and alive under the player's hands. My work lives where animation meets game feel — locomotion sets, combat, and everything that has to read in a sixtieth of a second.

I care about the craft of motion as much as the pipeline behind it: clean state machines, smart blending, and close collaboration with design and engineering to ship animation that plays as well as it looks.
